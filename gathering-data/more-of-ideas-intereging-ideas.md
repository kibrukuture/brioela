The agent notices you scanned 3 different chocolate bars at 11pm on a Tuesday. It doesn't lecture you. It just quietly remembers. Next time you're in that same pattern it might say "you usually want something sweet right now, there's a healthier option 200m from you." No form. No mood tracker. It just saw the pattern.

**2. It knows when you're sick before you do**
You've been scanning and eating certain things for 3 weeks. Suddenly your pattern breaks — you're buying ginger, honey, lemon. The agent already knows. It surfaces a recipe for what you actually need right now without you asking. Event-based. Triggered by behavior change, not a calendar.

**3. Pre-trip food intelligence**
You book a flight to Tokyo (it knows because you said "I'm going to Tokyo next week" out loud near the app or connected your calendar). Before you land it already knows what's healthy to eat there, what local products to scan, what the community notes say about food in that area. You land and it's already ready. Nobody built this.

**4. Ingredient rescue — live in the kitchen**
You open the fridge, point the camera, say nothing. It sees what's there. It tells you what you can cook right now with exactly those ingredients, ranked by how healthy it is for you specifically. No typing. No searching. Just point and it thinks.

**5. The "this is making you feel bad" pattern**
Over months the agent correlates what you eat with subtle signals — how often you cook after scanning something, whether you finish recipes, your scan time of day. It starts quietly surfacing: "you scan this brand a lot but almost never cook with it." Or more powerfully: "the last 4 times you ate heavily processed food you didn't cook for 3 days after." Pure behavioral pattern recognition. No questionnaire ever asked.

**6. Generational recipe resurrection**
You're on a live cook session with your grandmother. She's cooking from memory, no measurements, no steps, just doing it. The agent watches through the camera, listens, and reconstructs the full recipe in the background with estimated quantities.
