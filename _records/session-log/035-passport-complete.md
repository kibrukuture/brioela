## Product Direction Captured

- Name is Passport. Do not call it Food Passport or Mesa Passport.
- Passport is a temporary, privacy-safe real-world food instruction artifact.
- Passport is not a Discovery Card and not viral sharing.
- Passport communicates what must be respected to waiters, shoppers, caregivers, school staff, hosts, practitioners, or travel contexts.
- Passport expires and can be revoked.
- Passport includes only necessary food instructions, never a full private profile dump.
- Passport can be translated.
- Passport uses Generative Grammar for presentation, but validated instruction content remains static.
