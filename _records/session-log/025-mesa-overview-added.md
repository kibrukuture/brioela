- Name is Mesa. Do not call it Family Account or Household in user-facing product language.
- Mesa is the multi-person food audience layer: who is this food for, who can eat it, and what works for everyone at the table.
- Start with owner-managed Mesa before full multi-account auth.
- Future invited accounts can contribute selected scans/events into Mesa with permission.
- Invited accounts keep private Brioela memory private by default.
- Mesa can power grocery, pantry, meal planning, scanning, menu scanning, Bela substitutions, and cooking for more than one person.
- Mesa is likely an upgraded-tier/add-on feature, but pricing is undecided.
