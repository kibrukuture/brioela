# Music Prompt

Create a cinematic bed, not a song.

Direction:

- ancient warmth
- low drone
- ember/fire intimacy
- no trailer drums
- no obvious melody
- no modern tech ambience
- sparse handpan, krar, cello, or warm bowed texture
- one resolving note near Brioela wordmark arrival
- silence before the camera reveal

The music should hold emotion open without telling the listener what to feel.
