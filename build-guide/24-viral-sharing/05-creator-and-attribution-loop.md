# Viral Sharing — Creator And Attribution Loop

## What This File Covers

How verified_profile creators/chefs participate in sharing and recipe import without turning Brioela into an infinite video feed.

---

## Core Rule

Creator content spreads through utility, not scrolling.

The loop is:

1. Creator publishes or is attributed on a recipe/video.
2. User imports/cooks it in Brioela.
3. Brioela structures it, checks constraints, and helps cook it.
4. User can share a Discovery Card with creator attribution.
5. Creator gets distribution and trust without a feed.

---

## Attribution Card

Example:

```text
Cooked from [Creator] with Brioela.
Adapted for gluten-free automatically.
```

Rules:

- creator must be a `verified_profile` or source must be attributable
- adaptation details require user consent
- no private health profile exposure
- no creator access to user-specific allergy/condition data

---

## Creator Video Firewall

Viral Sharing depends on the Verified Profiles creator video firewall.

Rules:

- no infinite TikTok-like feed
- no autoplay creator feed
- no follower-ranking discovery
- videos surface only when relevant to current recipe, search, import, or cooking step
- if there are thousands of videos, show only the few that matter

This keeps Brioela focused on food decisions, not entertainment addiction.

---

## Brioela-Ready Creator Recipes

Verified_profile creators can eventually publish Brioela-ready recipe assets:

- ingredients
- steps
- timing
- allergens/constraints
- substitutions
- step markers in videos
- source attribution

This improves recipe import quality and gives creators a better sharing path.

---

## Creator Analytics Boundary

Creators can see aggregate privacy-safe performance later:

- recipe imports
- cook starts
- saves
- shares

Creators cannot see:

- user identities
- allergies
- medical conditions
- Mesa data
- wearable/glucose data
- private substitutions unless aggregated safely

---

## No Scraping Into Public Database

Verified creator attribution does not mean Brioela can scrape and republish all creator content.

Rules:

- user imports are private by default
- public creator recipes require creator-provided or licensed source
- attribution is preserved
- user-specific adaptations are private unless user shares a scrubbed card
