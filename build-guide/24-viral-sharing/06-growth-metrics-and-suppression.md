# Viral Sharing — Growth Metrics And Suppression

## What This File Covers

How Brioela measures organic growth, suppresses noisy sharing prompts, and avoids spammy growth mechanics.

---

## Metrics

Track:

- Discovery Card offer rate
- card preview rate
- share completion rate
- downstream install/open rate
- share sheet import conversion
- card type performance
- scan-to-share rate
- imported recipe later cooked
- creator attribution card engagement
- Mesa card engagement when Mesa ships

Do not optimize only for raw share volume. A smaller number of high-quality cards is better.

---

## Suppression

Sharing prompts should be sparse.

Rules:

- do not prompt after every scan
- do not prompt repeatedly for same card type after dismissal
- suppress if user dismisses share prompts twice in a week
- never push-notify to ask users to share
- never show share prompt before primary task is complete

---

## No Dark Growth

Blocked:

- financial referral programs at launch
- forced share to unlock features
- promotional push notifications
- streaks for sharing
- public leaderboards
- auto-tagging friends/family
- uploading contacts
- influencer payola as product mechanic

Brioela grows because moments are useful and surprising.

---

## 80-Day Build-In-Public Strategy

This is documented, not an in-app feature.

Content themes:

- scan surprising groceries
- ingredient transparency
- Brioela build process
- real product reactions
- recipe import demos
- menu scanning demos
- Kids Mode learning moments

The app should create the raw material for content. It should not become content spam.

---

## Quality Review

Review Discovery Cards periodically for:

- privacy leaks
- fear-based copy
- medical overclaims
- business-targeting language
- child identity leakage
- low-value promotional feel

If a card type repeatedly causes privacy risk or low-quality sharing, disable it until fixed.
