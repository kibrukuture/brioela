# Viral Sharing — Feature Specific Card Types

## What This File Covers

The specific Discovery Card types generated by Brioela features.

---

## Scan Discovery Card

Trigger: surprising product scan.

Examples:

```text
Found: 4 sugar cubes in one serving.
Scanned with Brioela.
```

```text
Found: this "healthy" snack has three artificial colors.
Scanned with Brioela.
```

---

## Swap Card

Trigger: Brioela suggests a better alternative.

Format:

```text
Instead of this...
Brioela suggested this.
Why: less sugar, no peanut risk, works for my table.
```

Must pass privacy scrub before showing any allergy/Mesa reason.

---

## Kids Learning Card

Trigger: Kids Mode explanation.

Example:

```text
We scanned this cereal together.
My kid learned ingredients are listed from most to least.
```

Never include child name or face by default.

---

## Mesa Compatibility Card

Trigger: Mesa finds a product/recipe/meal that works for selected audience.

Examples:

```text
Dinner that works for everyone at our table.
```

```text
5 people, 3 restrictions, 1 grocery list.
Found with Mesa.
```

Mesa owns the compatibility decision; Viral Sharing owns the privacy-safe card.

---

## Menu Reality Card

Trigger: menu scan produces a meaningful fit result.

Example:

```text
I scanned this menu.
Only 3 dishes fit my food profile.
```

Avoid public accusations. Keep it personal and factual.

---

## Recipe Preservation Card

Trigger: generational recipe captured or reconstructed.

Example:

```text
Preserved my grandmother's lentil soup with Brioela.
```

This is emotional, not promotional.

---

## Creator Recipe Card

Trigger: cooked/imported from a verified_profile creator/chef.

Example:

```text
Cooked from [Creator] with Brioela.
Adapted for gluten-free automatically.
```

Only include adaptation if privacy scrub and user consent allow it.

---

## Savings Card

Trigger: receipt/price intelligence finds a meaningful saving.

Example:

```text
Brioela found a cheaper version of my usual groceries.
Saved $18 this week.
```

Do not include full receipt or store path by default.

---

## Ground Find Card

Trigger: public Ground find is already approved by Ground gate.

Example:

```text
Fresh fenugreek spotted nearby today.
Found with Brioela Ground.
```

Only share finds that already passed Ground authenticity/privacy gate.

---

## Personal Response Card

Trigger: wearable/CGM insight.

Default: blocked.

Allowed only with explicit sensitive opt-in:

```text
I learned this snack spikes me more than I expected.
```

No medical claims, no raw glucose values by default.
