# 02 - Narration And Voice

## Goal

The voice should feel like an old truth being told directly to one person.

Not:

- trailer voice
- ad voice
- meditation voice
- product narrator
- tutorial narrator

Yes:

- close
- warm
- low
- unhurried
- ancient
- intimate
- certain
- sacred, not epic

## Recording Approach

Record/generate the whole script as one continuous performance.

Do not generate each line separately. The breath between lines is part of the emotional arc.

Use one master narration file:

```text
narration.mp3
```

Use cue data separately:

```text
narration-timestamps.json
timeline.json
```

## Voice Testing

Test multiple versions against these lines first:

```text
Before memory had words,
it had taste.
```

```text
Someone fed you.
```

```text
Brioela begins where language ends:
in the taste your body remembers
before your mind does.
```

If those three do not land, the voice is wrong.

## Production Rule

Voice first. Visuals follow voice. Music follows voice. Haptics follow voice.
