# 03 - Music Direction

## Goal

The music should hold emotion open without becoming a song.

It should feel like a warm cinematic bed under the narration.

## Avoid

- obvious melody
- sentimental piano everywhere
- trailer drums
- big heroic swell
- modern tech ambience
- bright pop chords

## Use

- low drone
- bowed texture
- warm air
- breath-like swell
- handpan / krar / cello / low resonant note
- silence as an instrument

## Music Arc

### Origin

- almost silence
- ember/fire carries the scene
- no melody

### Memory

- low drone grows
- warm harmonic enters
- subtle pulse begins

### Arrival

- one major note lands when Brioela arrives
- note decays naturally
- music leaves before camera takes over

## Rule

The music should not tell the user what to feel. It should make room for the feeling to appear.
