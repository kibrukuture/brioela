# Pricing Tiers — Tier Names And Copy

## What This File Covers

The public pricing names, emotional positioning, page headline, tier copy, and how the names map back to the older spec tiers.

---

## Naming System

Use these names:

| Product name | Spec equivalent | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Sapor | Free | start with food understanding |
| Luma | Core | private food memory and everyday intelligence |
| Culina | Chef | cooking companion and kitchen help |
| Viva | Power | full living Brioela experience |
| Mesa | new add-on/layer | more than one person at the table |
| Signet | B2B/Practitioner | verified people and businesses |

Do not use `Free`, `Core`, `Chef`, `Power`, or `B2B` as customer-facing plan names. They can remain internal aliases.

---

## Pricing Page Headline

Primary headline:

```text
From the food in front of you
to everyone at your table.
```

Subhead:

```text
Begin with the food in your hand. Go deeper when you want memory, cooking, Mesa, or verified tools.
```

Alternative headline if needed:

```text
How deeply should Brioela know your food?
```

## Trust Statement

Use this near pricing:

```text
We do not run ads. We do not sell your food life. We do not turn your health data into targeting.

Brioela is paid for by the people who want it to go deeper.
```

This should appear before or after the tier table. It is part of the pricing product, not footer copy.
