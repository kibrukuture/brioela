# Pricing Tiers — Tier Entitlements

## What This File Covers

What each named tier unlocks, what remains free forever, and which features belong to add-ons or future decisions.

---

## Free Forever

These are never gated:

- unlimited basic product scanning
- standard product verdict
- hard allergy guardrails
- boycott filters
- basic safety warnings
- core product confidence caveats

Pricing must never compromise safety or the scan-based discovery loop.

---

## Sapor — $0

Positioning:

```text
Begin with every product you wonder about.
```

Includes:

- unlimited basic product scans
- allergy and boycott guardrails
- basic verdicts
- basic product details
- read-only glimpses of map/community where available
- limited saved recipes/imports per spec baseline

Upgrade moments:

- wants deeper memory
- hits recipe save/import limit
- wants receipt intelligence
- wants Kids Mode/menu intelligence/Mesa/cooking

---

## Luma — $8/month

Positioning:

```text
Your private food memory.
```

Includes:

- everything in Sapor
- unlimited recipe saves/imports
- full private food memory
- receipt intelligence
- weekly food summary
- full healthy map and community notes
- menu scanning
- Kids Mode
- broader ambient food memory features that do not require live voice/video costs

Luma is the everyday food-intelligence tier.

---

## Culina — $24/month

Positioning:

```text
Cook with Brioela beside you.
```

Includes:

- everything in Luma
- voice cooking sessions within monthly limits
- fridge/pantry rescue
- generational recipe capture
- pre-trip food intelligence
- spend tracker/deeper receipt use
- deeper recipe help

Culina is where Brioela becomes active in the kitchen.

---

## Viva — $55/month

Positioning:

```text
The full living Brioela experience.
```

Includes:

- everything in Culina
- live video cooking
- multi-person cooking rooms
- advanced ambient intelligence
- advanced behavior reports
- priority routing where supported
- advanced wearable/CGM personalization if connected
- Mesa included

Viva is priced around expensive realtime voice/video usage and the fullest AI experience.

---

## Mesa — Add-On Included In Viva

Positioning:

```text
For everyone at your table.
```

Pricing:

```text
Mesa
+$8/month
Included in Viva
Up to 8 active Mesa members
```

Includes:

- multi-person Food Audience
- works-for-everyone product compatibility
- Mesa meal planning
- Mesa grocery compatibility
- invited contributor enrichment

Do not charge per active member within the 8-member limit.

---

## Signet — From $99/month

Positioning:

```text
Verified tools for people and food businesses.
```

Includes:

- verified_profile or verified_business
- verified map listing
- ingredient/menu transparency surfaces
- creator/chef recipe attribution
- practitioner/client tools where approved
- privacy-safe analytics

Signet is not a consumer tier. It is the verified person/business tier.
