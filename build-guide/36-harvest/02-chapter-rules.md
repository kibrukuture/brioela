## What This File Covers

What may become a chapter, and how chapters speak.

## Chapter Types

| Type | Example |
|---|---|
| firsts | "You discovered tahini in March. 14 recipes since." |
| avoidance maintained | "8 months without palm oil." |
| heritage | "Grandma's doro wat: captured in December, cooked 9 times." |
| discovery | "11 new cuisines this year." |
| craft | strongest growth-mirror arc (spec 53) |
| rhythm | most-cooked dish, best food month |
| family | Heirloom/Mesa moments at audience level only |

## Hard Exclusion List (candidate-layer enforcement)

Never a chapter, never a card: illness events, medical conditions, medications, glucose/biometric data, guest constraint details, craving history, locations finer than city. Enforced where candidates are created — downstream passes never see excluded material.

## Copy Rules

- Numbers from queries, not the model. Source refs mandatory per chapter.
- Observations only: "you did X." Never advice, never judgment, never "you should."
- Maintained change is observed, never scored — no streak language, no gamification framing (spec 38 doctrine).
- No comparison to other users, ever.

## Floors

- 6 strong chapters minimum or no edition.
- 10 active weeks of data minimum or no edition.

## Rule

A thin Harvest is worse than none. The floors are features.
