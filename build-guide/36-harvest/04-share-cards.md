# Harvest — Share Cards

## What This File Covers

The per-chapter share artifacts.

## Source Specs

- `brioela-specs/49-harvest.md`
- `brioela-specs/25-viral-growth-and-sharing.md`

## Card Rules

- One static card per chapter, pre-rendered at composition time (grammar Artifact Layer).
- Content: the chapter's headline fact + "my Harvest — Brioela." Nothing else.
- No location, no health-adjacent content — structurally guaranteed because excluded categories never became chapters.
- Looks like a personal moment, not an ad (the spec 25 law).
- EXIF/metadata stripped (same rule as Ground media).

## Sharing

- Per-card, explicit, one tap to the system share sheet. Brioela never posts anywhere itself. Nothing shares automatically.
- The cover card shares the whole-year summary.

## Attribution

Share cards carry a tagged install attribution path (distinct share-link tagging) so inheritance of the channel hypothesis is measurable: installs from Harvest cards tracked as their own acquisition source.

## Metrics To Instrument

open rate (7 days), completion rate, share rate (editions with ≥1 card shared; cards per sharing user), install attribution, 60-day retention delta opened vs. unopened, chapter-type share distribution (feeds next year's salience weights).
